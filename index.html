About Me

I am a full-stack engineer and lifelong musician based in St. Louis, Missouri. For nearly a decade, my world revolved around the technical architecture of sound. After earning a degree in Audio Production, I spent nine years working in pro audio and musical instrument sales, and high-end system design. From configuring custom setups for local artists and churches to designing complex audio infrastructure for major television networks, including The Late Show, my career has been built on solving intricate technical puzzles under tight deadlines.

Eventually, my passion for building and understanding how complex systems work led me from the mixing console to the text editor. I started teaching myself to code four years ago, and what began as a curious deep-dive quickly evolved into a dedicated pursuit of software engineering. To solidify my self-taught foundation and accelerate my transition into the tech industry, I joined the LaunchCode program, where I’ve been refining my collaborative workflows and full-stack capabilities.
